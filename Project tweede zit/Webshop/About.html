About Us

Welcome to "Toggle Center"
The Company was established in 2003. From the very beginning the Company tried to fill the gap in the area of every thing mobile related existing in the United States of America.
Through meetings with other managers and accountants I became more
convinced that the majority of webshops concerning mobiles specialized in a single direction only, and have difficulties in finding their own way in non-standard situations,
and one of the most important conditions for a business development is an advanced and quick in orientation accountant and legal advisor.
Thus, the Company was established with a pool of accountants and lawyers specialized in almost all the fields of economy.
The Company has a distinct division of areas of activity:
• Commerce
• IT
• Production
• Services
• Law
• Human Resource Management
Each area is divided into individual groups and subgroups, managed by corresponding specialists.
Staffing is essential for the companies that want to sell anything online as well as in stores. This problem is partially solved by trainings, arranged by our specialists and invited specialists and by employing experienced employees.
Today the Company has more than 150 employees with average age of 26 to 30, which allow us to be flexible in frequently changing legislative field, to follow the rhythm of the continuously developing business life, and thus, to meet the requirements of more than 1000 customers of the Company.
I can assure that the Company "Toggle Center" with its qualified staff and technical supplies offers high quality services in the area of accounting, corporate law, HR management, as well as advisory to businessmen in the field.
The major capital of our Company is our partners.
